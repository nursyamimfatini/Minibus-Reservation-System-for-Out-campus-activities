Welcome to Reserve-A-Ride!

Reserve-A-Ride is a web-based platform designed to make minibus reservations for out-campus activities quick and easy. With Reserve-A-Ride, you can easily book a minibus for your upcoming event or outing, without the hassle of traditional paper-based processes.

Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to select your preferred date and time, as well as desired destination. Once you've submitted your reservation request, admin will review and confirm your booking, and you'll be all set for your upcoming outing.

At Reserve-A-Ride, we're committed to providing a convenient, reliable, and comfortable solution for minibus reservations. With our platform, you can enjoy a hassle-free reservation experience. So why wait? Register today and start reserve minibus with Reserve-A-Ride!

  • Set the date and time as well as destination
  • Wait for confirmation.
